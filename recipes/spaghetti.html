Savory Spaghetti Sauce

Amazing and Simple Spaghetti Sauce

Zesty, savory, and terrifically meaty, this classic homemade Italian meat sauce recipe is perfect to enjoy with pasta and much more!

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Red onion
  • Garlic
  • Ground Italian sausage
  • Ground beef
  • Dried oregano
  • Canned tomato sauce
  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. First, set a heavy-bottomed 6-quart sauce pot over medium heat. Add the butter and onions, and stir until the onions become soft. If they start to brown, turn the heat down to medium-low.
  2. Then stir in the minced garlic and saute for another couple of minutes. Next add in the ground sausage, ground beef, and dried oregano. Cook the meat and break it into small pieces with a wooden spoon. Once the meat pieces are well browned, stir in the tomato sauce and crushed tomatoes.
  3. Cover and simmer for 30-60 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Once the sauce is done, stir in the fresh chopped basil. Taste, then salt and pepper as needed. If the meat sauce tastes too acidic, stir in 1-2 tablespoons sugar.
